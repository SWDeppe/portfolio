About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Swann, a web developer with a passion for both creating interactive websites and exploring the vast world of full-stack and game development. My journey started with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, but my goal is to dive deeper into back-end technologies and, ultimately, game development. I’ve worked on various web projects, including building a professional website for a photographer, Washingtoon. As I continue to grow my skills in full-stack development and game design, I’m excited to collaborate on projects that challenge me and push the boundaries of creativity.
